Week 3

In this post, I talk about how I can use my understanding of my Gregorc Thinkin Style to my advantage at DBC.

Abstract Random

My Gregorc Thinking Style is Abstract Random, which completely makes sense to me after looking more into it. I agree with this because I think that I work better when I discuss a problem with people. My experience in Phase 0 so far has been great, but something that would be better is working with other people in the same room. I feel that working with people online has been good so far, but will be better when we are together in the course. Realising this, I am thinking of going to meet ups to prepare myself, and to take advantage of my learning style.

Considering a fixed vs. growth mindset, I have been trying to apply this to other things in my life. I try not to be intimidated by challenges but encouraged by them, and I try to broaden my horizons on how I learn, and how I tackle a problem. By trying different things, I am able to understand more what is the best way for me to learn.
