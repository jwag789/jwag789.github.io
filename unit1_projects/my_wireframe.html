Here is the plan and wireframe for my index page.

The Plan

The plan for my web page is to make something very clean and simple. The design elements I want to play with are typography and negative space, and make something that should pop out. I want to put in links to my work, and eventually other elements, but I don't want to use up too much space, especially from the main "hero" section. I am thinking of using a small navbar, but I want the content container to be 100% for now, as to not see a difference between background colors. I plan to use icon fonts, and I plan to base the page on a fluid layout and make my page responsive.
