About me

Hello everyone! I am a graphic designer from New York. I was raised in Plainview on Long Island, and went to school at Northeastern University in Boston. Since then I've been living in Manhattan building webpages and doing design work. However, I want to expand my horizons and start building web apps. I want to build more complex and dynamic projects, and thought that this program would be a perfect fit for me.

Growing up, I've always loved playing video games with my brother, who is now a software engineer, working on popular games for mobile devices. I have always had the same interests as him, and after witnessing the culture and environment he is in as well as the work he does, I've grown extremely interested in immersing myself in that world.
I've always been interesting in learning to code, and now I am going to actually going to do something with that interest.
